Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some of the most common questions about caregiving.

Caregiving involves providing assistance to individuals who need help with daily activities due to age, illness, or disabilities. It can include tasks like bathing, feeding, transportation, and companionship.

Caregiving services are ideal for seniors, individuals recovering from surgery or illness, people with disabilities, and anyone who needs support with daily living activities.

If you or a loved one are struggling with daily tasks like cooking, bathing, or medication management, it may be time to consider professional caregiving services for safety and quality of life.

Yes, professional caregivers undergo training and certification to ensure they can provide safe and effective care. At Afnaan Care, our caregivers are carefully selected and trained.

To request caregiving services, simply contact us via phone, email, or WhatsApp, and our team will guide you through the process of assessing your needs and matching you with the right caregiver.
