AFNAAN CARE is a reputable Agency that provides Caregivers and Registered Nurses to care for the sick and vulnerable persons at their bedsides at home or hospital.

Our caregivers are in 3 categories depending on the condition of the care recipient or type of care:

  • Trained Caregivers (For all round personal care, protection and companionship)
  • Home Health Aides (Nursing Assistants) For mild conditions and all round personal care
  • Registered Nurses (For medical conditions. Doctor's prescribed and guide will be required).
    • Apart from personal and medical care, our caregivers also offer emotional and mental support for all round wellbeing of the care recipient.
